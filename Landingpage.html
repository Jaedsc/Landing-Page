PERSONAL

TRAINING

Workouts

Personal Training in a group setting. Led by an expert coach, the workouts focus on funtional strength, endurance and healthy range of motion. Groups are kept small, no more than 5. Since modifications are available, the workouts are great for all fitness and experience levels!

Nutrition

We provide a Dietician developed meal plan that includes recipes and a grocery list. You be able to meet with a trainer to discuss the plan, as well as practical strategies for implementing it into your daily life.

Accountability

You are never alone on your fitness journey! The biggest hurdle to success is always consistency. This is arguably the most important element in our training program. Having a coach that will be checking in on you and tracking your progress with you will keep your health at the forefront of your thoughts. Having planned workouts means that you just have to show up.

BASE

BURN

BUILD

All workouts are classified as either Base, Burn or Build. Base workouts utilize some strength movements, but emphasis balance and flexibility. Burn workouts use a variety of exercise types while striving to keep your heart-rate up. Build workouts are usually slower and more controlled. Heavier weights can be used, because strength is the primary focus.
